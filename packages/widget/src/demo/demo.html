Hover the tiny widget button (bottom-left by default — it scales up and reveals its label). Screenshots are on demand: the panel opens on the plain form, and Take screenshot captures this page (without the widget itself) and drops into the annotation editor.

Add ?position=bottom-right to flip the launcher side. In the editor, pick the Crop tool and drag a rectangle: the dimmed region is trimmed on submit, annotations stay editable and offset correctly, and Remove crop undoes it. Cancelling straight out of a fresh capture discards it.
