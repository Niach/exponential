The SVG icons in this directory are from Lucide (https://lucide.dev),
copied so the desktop app's ExpIcon set is self-contained (masterplan-v3
§4.7 — several glyphs the app needs are not in gpui-component's bundled
icon set). File names match the Lucide glyph names one-to-one.

Exceptions — two sign-in brand marks (EXP-9), not Lucide glyphs:
- apple.svg  — Apple logo glyph (path from Simple Icons, CC0), rendered
  monochrome on the "Sign in with Apple" button.
- google.svg — the official multi-color Google "G" from Google's
  sign-in branding assets, rendered full-color on the "Sign in with
  Google" button. Both are used nominatively to label their providers'
  sign-in buttons.
